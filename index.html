END (Efforts Never Die)

END (Efforts Never Die) Charitable Trust, is an Indian social development organization, is working for the education of deprived children and strengthening the financial and social capacities of needy families.

About

END (Efforts Never Die) Charitable Trust, have 3 Skill training centre for needy women in the slum resettlement colonies of Faridabad, we train 360 girls every year in Stitching & Tailoring, we are working in the districts of Nuh, Palwal and Haryana for imparting digital and skill education to children and youths. Our team is a mix of experts in legal assistance to poor, education, livelihood, and women’s empowerment in remote villages and urban slums.

The most important thing for a child is the education with dignity. It is the most powerful tool for social transformation. In fact it is an universal truth, a child cannot be educated in isolation.

Education enable the individuals to earn their bread and also increases one's knowledge on a range of issues. From good health, Parenting, emotional management for social behavior, to understanding one's rights – END (Efforts Never Die) Charitable Trust seeks to educate, empower and promote responsible, improved lifestyle of citizens.

Vision

The Childhood is the most valuable stage of human life and deserve to get the best emotional care, upbringing and child friendly environment to grow and excel.

Mission

END (Efforts Never Die) Charitable Trust begins with the coming together of an unlikely group of individuals, united in their passion to make an enduring contribution to the education system. END’s Vision is to enable local communities believe in their own capabilities to bring about change by providing them equal opportunity to quality education, good health, Improved lifestyle, with contributory efforts of community. A community with improved resourced to act for child development as whole.
